How to Play ☠️

Collect as many spooky emojis as you can:

  • 🦴 Bone = 1 point (always)
  • 💀 Skull = 3 points (unlocks at 3+ points)
  • 🧟 Zombie = 5 points (unlocks at 10+)
  • 🦇 Bat = 10 points (unlocks at 30+)
  • 🧛 Vampire = 25 points (unlocks at 50+)
  • 👻 Ghost = 1 point, floats randomly (bonus)

Milestones unlock faster spawns and new challenges. Try to reach 200 points!
