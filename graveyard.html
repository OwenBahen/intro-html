How to Play

Click on the bones 💀, skulls 💀, zombies 🧟, vampires 🧛, bats 🦇, and ghosts 👻 to collect points. Each type gives different points:

  • 🦴 Bone – 1 point
  • 💀 Skull – 2 points
  • 🧟 Zombie – 3 points
  • 🦇 Bat – 4 points
  • 🧛 Vampire – 5 points
  • 👻 Ghost – 1 point (clickable)

Reach milestones to unlock new messages and events on the grave!
